Performing the Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Framingham dataset and build a decision tree using different feature sets.

About the dataset

The dataset is publically available on kaggle website, and it is from an ongoing cardiovascular research which is taking place on the residents of Framingham city in Massachusettes,USA. The goal is to predict the 10-yrs risk of coronary heart disease. The Framingham dataset provides us with the patients information, with 4240 records and 15 attributes/columns.
