Employees Statistics - Retention
& Staff Turnover Report

The reports' goal is to gain a better insight of the company's employee retention and turnover rates via the use of a few graphs made from the collected data. In order to succeed in business, a corporation must recognise that its most precious asset is its workers. An organisation must ensure that after caring for its people, it is able to provide better productivity and customer service. As a result, management must decide whether employee retention and staff turnover rates are normal or worrying. This graph is intended to inform management of the conclusions of dataset from few perspectives in the hopes that management will get insight into the situation.
